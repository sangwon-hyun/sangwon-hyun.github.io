    About me

    CV / Github / Google Scholar / Orcid I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Statistics at the University of California, Santa Cruz. Before my current position, I was a post-doctoral researcher (advised by Jacob Bien), with a Ph.D. from Carnegie Mellon University (advised by Max G'Sell and Ryan Tibshirani) and a B.S. from the University of Michigan. I develop statistical tools for large complex data in real-world scientific applications. My current research centers around statistically analyzing the ocean's microbial ecology using high-resolution biological and remote sensing datasets (e.g., flow cytometry, metagenomics, DNA metabarcoding, and spatial proteomics).
